About

I am a researcher studying how environmental change affects marine species distributions and population dynamics. My work focuses on integrating habitat information with demographic processes using Bayesian statistical models.

My research interests include:

  • Species Distribution Models (SDMs)
  • Habitat-based Demographic Rate Models (DRMs)
  • Marine population dynamics
  • Climate change ecology

Research

Habitat Demographic Models

Modeling recruitment and survival as functions of environmental covariates using Bayesian approaches.

Species Distribution Models

Predicting habitat suitability and spatial distributions under climate change scenarios.

Ecological Data Science

Building reproducible workflows in R for ecological analysis, forecasting, and visualization.
